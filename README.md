# Social Poster : pxltr Studio

A high end, reactive social media post generator designed for consistent, premium aesthetics. This tool allows anyone to create perfectly framed 1:1 square posts with dynamic backgrounds that respond to the content of your message.

## Features

* Bulletproof Layout: Built with a massive safety margin and integrated footer to ensure social media algorithms never crop out your handle or details.
* Live Inline Editing: Click any text on the graphic : the Day, Progress, Date, Handle, or Message : to edit it directly.
* Reactive Engine: The background gradient automatically shifts colors based on your keywords:
    * Art / Design: Pink and Purple vibes.
    * Success / Done: Emerald Green vibes.
    * Alert / Warning: Sunset Amber vibes.
    * Minimal / Clean: Monochrome vibes.
* Auto Copy: Clicking "Prepare Screenshot" automatically copies your message to your clipboard for instant pasting into captions.
* Universal Access: Designed to be account agnostic so any creator can input their own handle and name.

## Usage

1. Open the tool at: https://jvhn0gl3.github.io/Social-poster/
2. Click on the text elements to customize your information.
3. Type your message in the central card to see the background react.
4. Click "Prepare Screenshot" to copy your text and enter fullscreen mode.
5. Take your screenshot and post.

## Technical Details

* Framework: HTML5, Tailwind CSS, FontAwesome.
* Typography: Inter and JetBrains Mono.
* Effects: Glassmorphism (backdrop filter) and CSS Radial Gradients.
